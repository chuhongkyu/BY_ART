BY_ART

BY_ART is a pixel art type. It consists of a total of 1024 pixels, 32 wide and 32 long. Although it is a fairly small amount of pixels, it expresses various characters. The biggest feature of BY_ART is that it is painted in yellow and blue colors. It shows a completely unique style, and this is the point that the artist has long been connected to paintings depicting yellow and blue people. Check and sympathize with the facial expressions of various characters. I recommend investing in his work.

Introduce the Artist

BY_ART Artist MR. Chu (Chu Hong-gyu) has learned oil painting from artist Kim Heung-soo at the Seoul Arts Center since elementary school. Knowing that he would major in Western painting, he entered the path of painting in Korean painting by choosing the only major in Korea called "Korean painting." And he took an unconventional step toward entering graduate school into graduate school of new media art. He has been painting from an early age to learn traditional paintings, but as he continues to challenge new technologies and paintings, he is now working through NFT art.
